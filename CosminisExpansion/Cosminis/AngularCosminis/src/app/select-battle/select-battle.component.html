How to Play
  • Battle a Random Friend or a Random stranger!
  • Life is a patch of bramble, why not put your savings on the line?
  • Pick the Cosmini that you want to send into battle!
  • LET THEM FIGHT! The most well-cared-for Cosmini shall be the victor.
  • You lose everything after losing two rounds.
  • The longer you go, the stronger the opponent, the bigger the cash-out.
  • You can cash out any time, it is okay, we understand that you are just poor.
